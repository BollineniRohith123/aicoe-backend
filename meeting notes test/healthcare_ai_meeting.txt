## Meeting Objective
Discuss and finalize requirements for implementing an AI-powered customer service automation system for our enterprise healthcare client. The system needs to handle patient inquiries, appointment scheduling, prescription refill requests, and billing questions while maintaining strict HIPAA compliance and providing seamless integration with existing Epic EHR system.

## Pre-Meeting Context
- Client has been using manual customer service for 15+ years
- Recent patient satisfaction survey showed 65% dissatisfaction with response times
- Current team of 25 CSRs handling 5000+ inquiries monthly
- Legacy phone system from 2018, no digital transformation yet
- Budget approved: $2.5M for Year 1 implementation

## Discussion Points

### Current Pain Points (Detailed Analysis)
- **Volume Overload:** Customer service team receives 200+ calls daily, with peak hours seeing 50+ concurrent calls
- **Repetitive Inquiries:** 70% of calls are about appointment scheduling, prescription status, and basic billing questions
- **Response Time Issues:** Average 24-hour response time for email inquiries, 45-minute hold times for phone calls
- **Staff Burnout:** 40% annual turnover rate, costing $300K+ in recruitment and training
- **Inconsistent Responses:** Different CSRs providing varying information, leading to patient confusion
- **After-Hours Coverage:** No 24/7 support, patients frustrated with limited hours
- **Language Barriers:** Growing Hispanic and Asian patient populations with limited multilingual staff
- **Integration Gaps:** No connection between phone system, EHR, and CRM systems

### Patient Journey Analysis
**Current Patient Experience:**
1. Patient calls main hospital line
2. Goes through IVR menu (press 1 for appointments, 2 for billing, etc.)
3. Waits on hold for 30-60 minutes
4. Speaks to CSR who manually looks up information in multiple systems
5. Gets response, but often needs to call back for follow-ups

**Desired Patient Experience:**
1. Patient initiates contact via preferred channel (phone, web, mobile app)
2. AI assistant immediately understands intent and provides instant responses
3. For complex issues, seamlessly transfers to human agent with full context
4. Follow-up communications automated and personalized

### Proposed Solution Architecture

#### Core Components
1. **Intelligent Virtual Assistant (IVA)**
   - Natural language processing for patient inquiries
   - Multi-language support (English, Spanish, Mandarin, Vietnamese)
   - Voice and text-based interactions
   - Integration with patient portal and mobile app

2. **AI-Powered Ticket Management System**
   - Automatic categorization of incoming inquiries
   - Priority scoring based on urgency and patient condition
   - Smart routing to appropriate departments/specialists
   - Automated resolution for routine requests

3. **Knowledge Base Integration**
   - Centralized repository of FAQs and procedures
   - Continuous learning from resolved tickets
   - Integration with medical literature and guidelines
   - Real-time updates from clinical staff

4. **Analytics and Reporting Dashboard**
   - Real-time performance metrics
   - Patient satisfaction tracking
   - Agent productivity analytics
   - Predictive workload forecasting

#### Integration Requirements
- **Epic EHR System:** Real-time patient data access (read-only)
- **JD Edwards ERP:** Billing and financial data integration
- **Microsoft Dynamics CRM:** Customer relationship management
- **Twilio Communications:** Multi-channel messaging platform
- **Azure Cognitive Services:** AI/ML capabilities
- **Azure Health Bot:** Healthcare-specific conversational AI

### Technical Requirements (Detailed)

#### Security & Compliance
- **HIPAA Compliance:** End-to-end encryption, audit trails, data minimization
- **HITRUST Certification:** Required for healthcare data handling
- **Zero Trust Architecture:** Multi-factor authentication, least privilege access
- **Data Residency:** All patient data must remain within US borders
- **Backup & Recovery:** 99.999% uptime SLA, 15-minute RTO

#### Performance Requirements
- **Response Time:** <2 seconds for AI responses, <5 seconds for system queries
- **Availability:** 99.9% uptime, 24/7 operation
- **Scalability:** Handle 10,000 concurrent users, auto-scaling capabilities
- **Accuracy:** >95% intent recognition accuracy
- **Languages:** Support for 5+ languages with medical terminology

#### System Integration
- **API Requirements:** RESTful APIs with OAuth 2.0 authentication
- **Data Formats:** FHIR for health data, JSON for general communications
- **Real-time Sync:** Sub-second data synchronization across systems
- **Legacy System Support:** Adapters for older systems without modern APIs

### Business Requirements (Detailed)

#### Functional Requirements
- **Automated Appointment Scheduling:** Book, reschedule, cancel appointments
- **Prescription Management:** Refill requests, status checks, delivery tracking
- **Billing Inquiries:** Explanation of charges, payment processing, insurance claims
- **Medical Record Requests:** Secure delivery of test results and records
- **Emergency Handling:** Immediate escalation for urgent medical situations
- **Feedback Collection:** Automated patient satisfaction surveys
- **Multi-channel Support:** Phone, web chat, SMS, email, mobile app

#### User Experience Requirements
- **Intuitive Interface:** Simple, clean design following healthcare UX guidelines
- **Accessibility:** WCAG 2.1 AA compliance for all users
- **Mobile-First:** Responsive design for all device types
- **Voice Commands:** Support for hands-free operation in clinical settings
- **Progressive Disclosure:** Show information as needed to reduce cognitive load

#### Operational Requirements

- **Monitoring & Support:** 24/7 technical support and system monitoring
- **Continuous Improvement:** Regular updates based on user feedback and performance data
