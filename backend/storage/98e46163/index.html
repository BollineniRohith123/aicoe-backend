Fitness Tracking App

E2E Test Documentation - A comprehensive fitness tracking solution designed for beginners, featuring AI-powered nutrition tracking, workout logging, and progress visualization.

Project Overview

Meeting Details

Date: January 15, 2025

Objective: Define core features, target audience, technical stack, and monetization strategy

Attendees: Sarah (PM), Mike (Tech Lead), Jessica (Designer), Tom (Marketing)

Target Audience

Fitness beginners aged 25-45 who feel overwhelmed by complex fitness apps

Focus on simplicity and ease of use

Mobile-first platform strategy

Key Decisions

• Freemium model at $9.99/month

• React Native, Node.js, PostgreSQL, AWS

• AI-powered photo nutrition tracking

• 6-8 month timeline

Pain Points

• Competition with large companies

• High infrastructure costs

• AI accuracy concerns

• User overwhelm with complex apps
