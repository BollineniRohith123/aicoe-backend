Executive Summary

The Ecommerce Platform project aims to develop a comprehensive, enterprise-grade digital commerce solution that leverages industry-leading technologies and best practices to deliver exceptional user experiences and drive business growth. The platform will feature AI-powered personalization, mobile-first responsive design, omnichannel fulfillment capabilities including BOPIS, and a frictionless checkout process to compete with market leaders like Shopify and Amazon. With a focus on scalability, security, and compliance with GDPR, CCPA, and PCI DSS standards, the platform will serve both guest users and registered customers across web and mobile channels. Key success metrics include achieving a 35% conversion rate, reducing cart abandonment to below 20%, and increasing Average Order Value (AOV) by 25% through personalized recommendations and cross-selling features. The strategic importance lies in establishing a competitive digital presence that can adapt to evolving market trends including headless commerce, social commerce integration, and progressive web app capabilities.

Business Objectives

Primary Objectives

Launch a fully functional ecommerce platform within 9 months that achieves 35% conversion rate, supports 100,000+ concurrent users, processes $10M in annual revenue, and integrates with existing ERP and CRM systems. Implement AI-powered personalization to increase customer retention by 30% and achieve 99.9% uptime with sub-2-second page load times.
