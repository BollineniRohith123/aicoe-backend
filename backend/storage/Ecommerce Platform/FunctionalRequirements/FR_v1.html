Functional Overview

The Ecommerce Platform is a comprehensive, enterprise-grade online shopping solution designed to deliver exceptional user experiences through AI-powered personalization, mobile-first responsive design, and omnichannel fulfillment capabilities. The platform supports both guest and registered customers, featuring frictionless checkout, advanced product discovery, self-service account management, and automated returns processing. Built with modern architectural patterns including PWA capabilities and headless commerce principles, the system ensures high performance, security compliance with PCI DSS, GDPR, and CCPA standards, and accessibility adherence to WCAG 2.1 guidelines.

User Stories

US-001

AI-Powered Product Discovery

High Priority
8 story points

As a shopper, I want to see personalized product recommendations and search results so that I can discover relevant products quickly and increase my likelihood of making a purchase.

Acceptance Criteria

  • Given I am on the homepage When I view the page Then I see personalized product recommendations based on my browsing history and preferences
  • Given I am using the search bar When I type a query Then I receive real-time autocomplete suggestions for products, categories, and attributes
  • Given I am on a category page When I apply filters Then the product grid updates dynamically without page reload
  • Given my search returns no results When the search completes Then I see alternative suggestions and similar products

Business Value

Increases user engagement and conversion rates through relevant product discovery, directly impacting AOV and customer loyalty

US-002

Comprehensive Product Evaluation

High Priority
5 story points

As a shopper, I want to view detailed product information with rich media and real-time inventory so that I can make informed purchase decisions and trust the platform.

Acceptance Criteria

  • Given I am viewing a product When the page loads Then I see high-resolution images, 360-degree view, and product videos
  • Given I am viewing a product When I select variants Then the price and image update dynamically
  • Given I am viewing a product When inventory is low Then I see a real-time stock indicator
  • Given I am viewing a product When BOPIS is available Then I see nearby store availability and pickup times

Business Value

Builds customer trust and reduces purchase hesitation, directly impacting conversion rates

US-003

Frictionless Checkout Experience

High Priority
13 story points

As a shopper, I want to complete my purchase quickly and securely with minimal steps so that I can avoid cart abandonment and have a positive purchasing experience.

Acceptance Criteria

  • Given I have items in my cart When I start checkout Then I see a single-page checkout interface
  • Given I am a returning customer When I checkout Then I can use one-click purchase with saved information
  • Given I am checking out When I select payment Then I see multiple secure payment options including digital wallets
  • Given I apply a promo code When I submit it Then the order total updates instantly with the discount
