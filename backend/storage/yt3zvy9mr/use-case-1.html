Use Case Summary

Use Case ID
UC-001
Title
Conduct Market Research
Description
Systematically gather and analyze market data to inform product development decisions for Test V3
Priority
High

Involved Actors

Product Manager
Responsible for defining research objectives and making product decisions based on findings
Market Research Analyst
Conducts primary and secondary research, analyzes data, and generates insights
Business Analyst
Supports data analysis and helps translate findings into actionable recommendations

Main Flow

1
Define research scope and objectives
2
Identify target market segments
3
Select appropriate research methodologies
4
Execute primary and secondary research
5
Collect and analyze data
6
Generate insights report
7
Present findings to stakeholders
8
Document actionable recommendations

Alternate Flows

A1
Identify data gaps
A2
Adjust research methodology
A3
Seek alternative data sources
A4
Document limitations in findings

Preconditions

Research objectives are defined
Budget for research tools is allocated
Research team is available

Postconditions

Market research report is completed
Stakeholders are aligned on market insights
Product decisions are informed by data

Business Value

Risk Reduction
Reduces the risk of building unwanted features by validating market needs early
Market Alignment
Ensures product development aligns with actual market needs and customer expectations
Data-Driven Decisions
Provides quantitative and qualitative data to support strategic product decisions
Cost Efficiency
Optimizes resource allocation by focusing development efforts on validated opportunities
